About Us

Here in Petite Treats, our bakers are exceptionally trained and our head baker is an artisan-baker who was trained in Paris for 6 years!
Our bakery is located at a great beautiful location near the Willows Shopping Centre. Our bakery shop is a great hit as we have our loyal customers from James Cook University, the Cowboys and Telstra!
